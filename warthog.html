WARTHOG

Warthog is a wild member of the pig family,in the past , it was commonly treated as a subspecies of P.aethiopicus, but today that scientific name is restricted to the desert warthog of northen Kenya. Somalia and eastern Ethopia

The head of the common warthog is large , with a mane down the spine to the middle of the back,spares hair covers the body .it is usuallyblack or brown.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM?

they can be found in grassland, savanna and woodland in sub-suharan Africa.
