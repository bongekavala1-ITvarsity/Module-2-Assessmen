PANDA

Panda is a bear native to south central China. It is characterised by large,black patches around its eye,over the ears, and across

its round body.

Pandas do not hibernate.When winter approaches, they head lowerdown their mountain homes to warmer temperatures, where they continue to chomp away on bamboo.
